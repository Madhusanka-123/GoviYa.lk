Process

Paddy Cultivation Stages

Understanding the complete lifecycle of rice cultivation from preparation to harvest

01

🌱 Land Preparation

Plowing, leveling, and preparing the field for planting. This includes proper water management, soil testing, and bund construction for optimal growing conditions.

⏱️ Duration: 2–3 weeks
02

🌾 Seed Selection & Sowing

Choosing the right paddy variety based on climate, soil type, and seasonal conditions. Proper seed treatment, pre-soaking, and sowing techniques for uniform germination.

⏱️ Duration: 1–2 weeks
03

💧 Growth & Maintenance

Regular monitoring, balanced fertilizer application, integrated pest and disease management, and smart water management throughout the growing season.

⏱️ Duration: 8–10 weeks
04

🌿 Flowering & Grain Filling

Critical stage requiring careful water management and protection from pests. Proper nutrition at this stage directly impacts grain quality and final yield.

⏱️ Duration: 3–4 weeks
05

🚜 Harvesting

Optimal timing for harvesting, proper mechanized or manual techniques, and post-harvest handling to minimize losses and maintain premium grain quality.

⏱️ Duration: 1–2 weeks
